Christine Rose

Christine Rose is an award-winning author of fantasy fiction, both in the long and short form, a blogger, and a podcaster. She has eleven books published, over two dozen short stories, and hundreds of articles. She also wrote under the pen name O.M. Grey and appeared at several Steampunk conventions as a literary guest of honor. As a public speaker, she's covered topics like publishing & marketing realities for the emerging author, sexual violence and safety at genre conventions, and incorporating history into fantasy fiction.

Christine now leads a team of talented, skilled writers at Radically Open Security and Lead Writer and Global Program Manager, keeping track of all the projects and assignments in addition to her writerly duties.

She enjoys snowshoeing the the beautiful silence of a winter morning, and she prefers grey, rainy days while wandering through London's historical streets. Apart from work, she travels as a tech-nomad, moving from place to place petsitting and exploring the magnificent beauty of the world.

She's fueled by copious amounts of dark chocolate, frothy mochas, and silent solitude.
