Marcus Bointon

Marcus gained his MSc in interactive computer system design at Loughborough University of Technology in 1994, gaining the only distinction awarded for his thesis, and a BSc in Computer Science and Digital Electronics from King's College London. He maintains PHPMailer, a top-10 PHP project on GitHub and he is a a regular contributor to innumerable other open-source projects, including Apache, Zend Framework, LESS, Twitter Bootstrap and HTMLPurifier and wrote the WHATWG HTML5 specification for email addresses.
