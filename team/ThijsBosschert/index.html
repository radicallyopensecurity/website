Thijs Bosschert

Since 2015 Thijs Bosschert is a freelance security professional working for multiple companies in all facets of security. Before starting as a freelancer Thijs worked at Deloitte, Verizon Business and Fox-IT. Thijs has 15 years of experience in IT security including 10 years of experience in Incident Response and Forensics. Thijs his knowledge further includes infrastructure pentesting, web application pentesting, red teaming, wireless security, monitoring, security research, social engineering, reverse engineering, hardware and malware analysis. Thijs also provides training and public presentations in the fields of his knowledge and holds multiple certifications including CISSP, ACE, OSCP and OSWP. Thijs is a core and founding member of the Eindbazen CTF team, and is also part of the Hack.ERS CTF team that won the Global CyberLympics in 2012 & 2013.

2012 - 2015 Deloitte

Thijs has been the Manager of the Incident Response team of Deloitte and was responsible for cyber security, cyber forensics and incident response. Besides leading the incident response team Thijs has built the team from the ground up and trained all the people in the team. Next to training the incident response team, Thijs has also been training over 50 people of the Deloitte Hacking team.

2008 - 2012 Verizon Business

Before joining Deloitte Thijs was a Principal Consultant with the Forensic and Investigative Team at Verizon Business. He has held this role since August 2008. In this role, he was responsible for executing and managing incident response and forensic investigations in the EMEA region including payment card data breaches, industrial espionage and custom malware analysis. Thijs was one of the authors and contributors of the Verizon Databreach Investigations Report.

2005 - 2008 Fox-IT

From 2005 to July 2008 Thijs was a forensic investigator at Fox-IT, a Dutch company specializing in state-secret level encryption products and forensics. In this role Bosschert was responsible for performing high profile forensic IT investigations for various large international companies in multiple sectors including the financial sector. This included reporting and presenting the outcome of the investigations and advising customers on preventive measures and follow-up actions post investigation. He was also involved in conducting IT security and social engineering audits and in addition worked on forensic innovation and research projects.
