Deborah Maaijen

Deborah has been in to Open Source software for over 15 years, regularly visits Free and Open Source conferences and meetings like T-Dose, NLLGG, OWASP and FOSDEM and is a big GNU/Linux advocate. She also made her way from Linux helpdesk jobs to a system administrator and a Python programmer and Java webdeveloper position.

Alongside these jobs, she volunteered for the Free Software Foundation Europe and as a co-organizer of meetings for the Dutch Linux User Group. Besides Open Source, she is also interested in the world of digital privacy and videogames.
