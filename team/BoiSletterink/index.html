Boi Sletterink

Boi is a freelance IT security professional, working with companies in all facets of security.

Before starting as an independent contractor in 2014, he worked for Fox-IT, turning his long-standing hobby into his profession. Boi has 10 years of experience providing VPN networks to customers with high-security environments, in all steps of the process from pre-sales to support.

Other fields Boi is knowledgeable about include cryptography, forensics, security assessment, pentesting, network security, host security, secure software development, Common Criteria, security management and regulations surrounding governmentally classified information.

Boi also provides training in the fields of his knowledge. Boi has also volunteered for several security conferences, including HITBAMS2015.
