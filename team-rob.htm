Rob Wiegertjes

Rob Wiegertjes is a physical security enthusiast for over 10 years and competitive lockpicker for almost 6. He has taught lockpicking to hundreds of people all over the Netherlands and a larger part of the United States. He also contributed to several lockpicking events and villages, amongst them SXSW, BIL conference, OHM, Hack in the Box and BsidesSF.
